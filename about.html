The Story

A Passion for
Extraordinary Travel

Trips should fit the way you live, not the other way around. I build each one around your pace, your habits, and the small preferences that make a holiday feel like yours. By the time you arrive, I want the logistics to feel settled so you can simply enjoy being there.

I’m affiliated with Coastline Travel Group, which opens the door to stronger amenities, priority recognition where it matters, and relationships with partners around the world. A lot of what comes with that is simply not the kind of thing you’d match on your own from a booking site.

I spend part of my year in Portugal and part in the United States, and I work with clients wherever they are. You have one person beside you from the first spark of an idea to the day you’re home again.
